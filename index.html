About Headway

Headway Projects is a proudly South African company offering four distinct but complementary services: professional website creation and maintenance for SMEs, bulk logistics through side tipper trucks and water tankers including direct water supply, air-conditioning and refrigeration materials, equipment and installation, and a full-spectrum printing and branded apparel service covering DTF, sublimation, screen printing, and embroidery. One company, four solutions, zero compromise.
